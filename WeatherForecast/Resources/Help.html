How to use Weather app

1. How to watch forecast?

To view forecast for a city, add it using "plus" button on main screen.

2. How to choose the city on a map?

Press "Drop Pin" button or tap on map to drop the pin at the city. Than press "Bookmark" button to save it.

3. How to delete bookmarked location?

Swipe left on a location to delete it.

4. How to close help?

Swipe down to close the screen.
